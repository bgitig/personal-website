This project is to be my personal website for displaying academic work, personal projects, and creative works.

Structure:
I want the frontend to be a single fullscreen, non-scrollable bookshelf populated by books and objects. Shelves should be labeled as "personal" "creative" and "professional". Every reload/everytime someone visits it, I want it to look different, meaning book size, color, and order/position, should be random and object shelf and position should also be random.

Examples of objects include: plants (filler), paintings (should hold a real picture that i will upload, should be clickable), globes, chessboard/piece (should also be clickable), etc.

Interactivity:
books should slightly come off the shelf toward you when the mouse hovers on them. clicking a book

Visual tone:
I would like the website feeling to be dark, gloomy, archaic, and musty like an old bookstore. I do NOT want it to look standard and "vibe coded." I want it to look gritty and detailed. Books should  be dark earth tones like browns, reds, greens. The bookshelf should be dark wood. I would like the bookshelf to be textured in some way and not a flat color (this does not have to be randomized). Books should also not be a flat color, but have some mottling or depth shadowing. Most of the webpage should be very dark, and there should be a circle of light around the mouse. as the mouse moves, the light travels with it, illuminating the dimmer background, as if moving the mouse is moving a candle.
